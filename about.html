About FreightPro

Your trusted partner in freight brokering and logistics solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2010, FreightPro Logistics began as a small family business with a simple mission: to make freight brokering more transparent, reliable, and cost-effective for businesses of all sizes.

What started as a local operation has grown into a trusted national partner, handling thousands of shipments annually while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that sets us apart.

Today, we're proud to serve clients across diverse industries, from manufacturing and retail to technology and healthcare, providing customized logistics solutions that drive their success.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional freight brokering services that connect businesses with reliable carriers, optimize supply chains, and deliver value through transparency, efficiency, and innovation.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative freight brokering partner, setting the standard for excellence in logistics solutions and customer service.

Our Values

Integrity, reliability, transparency, and customer-first approach guide everything we do. We build lasting partnerships based on trust and mutual success.
