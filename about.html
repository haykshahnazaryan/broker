About Aurus Logistics INC

Your trusted partner in freight brokering and logistics solutions

Our Story

2018

The Beginning

Aurus Logistics INC was founded with a vision to revolutionize freight brokering. Starting as a small family business in Waltham, Massachusetts, we recognized the need for more transparent, reliable, and cost-effective logistics solutions. Our founders, driven by decades of experience in transportation and logistics, set out to bridge the gap between shippers and carriers.

2020

Rapid Growth

Despite global challenges, we expanded our network to include over 2,000 trusted carriers nationwide. Our commitment to 24/7 customer support and innovative technology solutions helped us serve clients across diverse industries, from manufacturing and retail to technology and healthcare.

2022

Technology Integration

We invested heavily in cutting-edge logistics technology, implementing real-time tracking systems, automated dispatch solutions, and data analytics platforms. This technological advancement allowed us to provide unprecedented visibility and efficiency to our clients.

2024

Industry Leadership

Today, Aurus Logistics INC stands as a trusted industry leader, handling thousands of shipments annually while maintaining the personal touch that sets us apart. Our Premier Gold Network includes over 100 top-tier carriers, and our team of 50+ trained specialists ensures every shipment receives the attention it deserves.
