Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I get a freight quote?

We typically provide quotes within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent shipments, we can provide same-day quotes. Emergency requests are handled 24/7.

What types of freight do you handle?

We handle all types of freight including dry van, reefer, flatbed, LTL, FTL, and specialized loads. Our network covers general freight, hazardous materials, oversized loads, and temperature-controlled shipments.

Do you provide cargo insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive cargo insurance coverage through our network of trusted insurance providers. We can arrange full-value coverage for your shipments with competitive rates.

How do you ensure carrier reliability?

All carriers in our network undergo rigorous screening including safety ratings, insurance verification, and performance history. We continuously monitor carrier performance and maintain relationships with only the most reliable operators.

Can you handle international shipments?

Currently, we specialize in domestic freight within the United States and Canada. For international shipments, we can refer you to our trusted international logistics partners.
